Coal is used for about 30% of world,s power production and about ____ of pakistan,s power production.

(A) 30%

(B) 40%

(C) ** 10%

(D) 50%

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Currently, the overall electricity generation from coal has reached to 5280 MW. Thar coal is contributing 1, 320 MW, while imported coal contribution in electricity generation is 3, 960 MW which is around 75 percent of the total electricity generation from coal in the country.

Concept note-2: -Coal supplies just over a third of global electricity generation, and until newer technologies are available will continue to play a crucial role in industries such as iron and steel. The IEA’s Net Zero Emissions by 2050 Scenario envisions that all unabated coal generation ends by 2040.

Concept note-3: - Ministry of Coal However, Major production of Electricity is achieved through coal a thermal power plant which is around 75% of the total power generation.
