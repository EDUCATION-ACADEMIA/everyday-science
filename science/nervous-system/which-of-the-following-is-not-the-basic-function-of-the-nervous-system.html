Which of the following is not the basic function of the nervous system?

(A) detects and senses stimuli

(B) responds to sensory stimulation

(C) sends signal rapidly between body parts

(D) ** produces fluids such as plasma and intestinal fluid

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Which of the following is NOT one of the basic functions of the nervous system? Release hormones into the bloodstream to communicate with other cells in the body.

Concept note-2: -Your nervous system is your body’s command center. Originating from your brain, it controls your movements, thoughts and automatic responses to the world around you. It also controls other body systems and processes, such as digestion, breathing and sexual development (puberty).

Concept note-3: -The nervous system transmits signals between the brain and the rest of the body, including internal organs. In this way, the nervous system’s activity controls the ability to move, breathe, see, think, and more.

Concept note-4: -The nervous system has 3 main functions: sensory, integration, and motor. Sensory. The sensory function of the nervous system involves collecting information from sensory receptors that monitor the body’s internal and external conditions.
