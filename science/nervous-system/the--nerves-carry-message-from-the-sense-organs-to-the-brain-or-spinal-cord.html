The ____ nerves carry message from the sense organs to the brain or spinal cord.

(A) Motor

(B) ** Sensory

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -For example, sensory neurons send information from the eyes, ears, nose, tongue, and skin to the brain. Motor neurons carry messages away from the brain to the rest of the body to allow muscles to move. These connections make up the way we think, learn, move, and feel.

Concept note-2: -The thalamus carries messages from the sensory organs like the eyes, ears, nose, and fingers to the cortex.

Concept note-3: -Sensory neurons carry information from the sense organs (such as the eyes and ears) to the brain. Motor neurons control voluntary muscle activity such as speaking and carry messages from nerve cells in the brain to the muscles.
