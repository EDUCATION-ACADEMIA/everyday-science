SCIENCE

NERVOUS SYSTEM

[SOURCES]
The purpose of the Nerouns

(A) Insulate the axon fibers increase rate of speed

(B) Collect Stimuli

(C) ** Transmit information in the form of nerve impulses through the body

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Neurons are the functional unit of the nervous system. They have this amazing ability to detect, receive and transmit a variety of stimuli. These neurons communicate with each other through nerve impulses, which can be understood scientifically.

Concept note-2: -Sensory neurons carry nerve impulses from sense organs and internal organs to the central nervous system. Motor neurons carry nerve impulses from the central nervous system to organs, glands, and muscles-the opposite direction.

Concept note-3: -The nerve cell, or neuron, is the key player in the activity of the nervous system. It conveys information both electrically and chemically. Within the neuron itself, information is passed along through the movement of an electrical charge (i.e., impulse).

Concept note-4: -Neurons are information messengers. They use electrical impulses and chemical signals to transmit information between different areas of the brain, and between the brain and the rest of the nervous system.

Concept note-5: -Tightly controlling membrane resting potential is critical for the transmission of nerve impulses.
