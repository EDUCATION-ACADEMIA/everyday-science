This contains a variety elements and compounds that ARE NOT chemically combined.

(A) Water

(B) ** Mixture

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Mixtures contain different elements and compounds but the ratio is not fixed nor are they combined via chemical bonds. The ingredients are physically mixed but chemically separate. Often they are visibly distinct.

Concept note-2: -A mixture contains a variety of elements and compounds that are not chemically combined.

Concept note-3: -A mixture is made when two or more substances are combined, but they are not combined chemically. There are two main categories of mixtures: homogeneous mixtures and heterogeneous mixtures.

Concept note-4: -A mixture is a physical combination of two or more substances. These are not combined chemically. The components retain their individual properties even after forming a mixture.

Concept note-5: -An element is a substance that consists of only one type or kind of atom. An element is a pure substance as it cannot be broken down or transformed into a new substance even by using some physical or chemical means.
