If you add oil to water and shake the liquid, you will form a?

(A) ** heterogeneous mixture

(B) pure substance

(C) miscible liquid

(D) cupcake

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -If one tries to mix water and oil then no matter how much you shake the liquid, they never mix well together, hence they form a immiscible liquid and a heterogeneous mixture because water and oil are not evenly distributed.

Concept note-2: -Hence, a mixture of oil and water is a heterogeneous mixture.

Concept note-3: -Hence, oil in water is a heterogeneous mixture.

Concept note-4: -However if oil and water are shaken together, droplets of one liquid will mix into the other. This mixture is called an emulsion.

Concept note-5: -The liquid that is immiscible form heterogeneous mixtures. A good example is a mixture of oil and water.
