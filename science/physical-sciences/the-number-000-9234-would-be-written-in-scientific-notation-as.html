The number .000 9234 would be written in scientific notation as

(A) 9.234 x 10-3

(B) 9.234 x 10-1

(C) 9.234 x 10-7

(D) ** 9.234 x 10-4

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In this case that would be 9, and we have to move the decimal to the left 3 times to get 0.009 . So our final answer is 9⋅10−3 .

Concept note-2: -0.0000008 written in scientific notation is 8 × 10 –7.

Concept note-3: -Convert from Real Number to Scientific Notation: 0.000345 = 3.45 × 10.

Concept note-4: -Answer: The scientific notation for 0.0001 is 1 × 10-4. All the numbers written in scientific notation are between 1 and 10 multiplied with powers of 10.
