____ is anything that has mass and takes up space.

(A) property

(B) ** matter

(C) solution

(D) mass

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Matter is anything that has mass and occupies space.

Concept note-2: -Matter is anything that has mass and takes up space. It includes molecules, atoms, fundamental particles, and any substance that these particles make up. Matter can change form through physical and chemical changes, but through any of these changes matter is conserved.

Concept note-3: -Matter is any substance that is made of atoms and takes up space. There are several different forms that matter can be found in, and these are solid, liquid, gas, and plasma.

Concept note-4: -The statement is true. Matter is anything that has mass and occupies space. Ex: air, water, rock.

Concept note-5: -According to the general definition, matter is anything that occupies space (having volume) and has mass. On the other hand, mass is a quantity that measures of the quantity of matter in a particular object, particle, or space.
