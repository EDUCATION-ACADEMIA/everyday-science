Two copies of the same book are in a bookcase. One book is twice as high as the other. They have the same potential energy

(A) True

(B) ** False

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A book sitting on a shelf in the library is said to have potential energy because if it is nudged off the shelf, gravity will accelerate the book, giving the book kinetic energy.

Concept note-2: -Potential energy is stored energy and the energy of position. Chemical energy is energy stored in the bonds of atoms and molecules.

Concept note-3: -Answer and Explanation: The answer is 1. A reaction cannot be exothermic overall if activation energy is required. The statement is false because reactions can be exothermic overall even if activation energy.
