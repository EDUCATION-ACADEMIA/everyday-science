EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The main reason to study physics is because you are curious about how the world works. In addition to that knowledge, you gain a set of incredibly useful skills that make you attractive to a wide range of employers. A physics degree trains you to become an expert problem solver.

Concept note-2: -Physical science is an exciting intellectual adventure that inspires young people. It expands our knowledge about Nature. Physical science have created fundamental knowledge that is needed for the future technological advances which leads to the continuity of the economic engines of the world.

Concept note-3: -Physical science is the study of the inorganic world. That is, it does not study living things. (Those are studied in biological, or life, science.) The four main branches of physical science are astronomy, physics, chemistry, and the Earth sciences, which include meteorology and geology.

Concept note-4: -Modern Technology. Understanding the Universe. The Complex Mathematics that is actually interesting! The basic understanding of how things move.
