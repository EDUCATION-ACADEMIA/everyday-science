Which of the following is true about acids and bases?

(A) ** The lower the pH, the stronger the acid

(B) the higher the pH, the stronger the acid

(C) The lower the pH, the more neutral the acid

(D) The higher the pH, the weaker the base

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -So, the higher the pH value, the weak is the acid while the higher the pH value for a base means a stronger base.

Concept note-2: -Answer: a)The lower the pH, the stronger the acid is the correct answer.

Concept note-3: -Acids mixed with bases neutralize each other. The explanation for the correct option (B): When an acid reacts with a base, it neutralizes to produce salt and Water ( H 2 O ). This reaction is known as a neutralization reaction.

Concept note-4: -Lower pH number means stronger acid, higher pH number means stronger base. It can be a little confusing, but the lower the pH of a substance is, the stronger of an acid it is.

Concept note-5: -Hence, option (C) is correct., sour and changing blue litmus to red is true for acids.
