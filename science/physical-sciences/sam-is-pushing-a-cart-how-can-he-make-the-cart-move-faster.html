Sam is pushing a cart. How can he make the cart move faster?

(A) He can add weight to the cart.

(B) He can push the cart up a ramp.

(C) He can take the wheels off the cart

(D) ** He can increase the force of his push.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The girl is using her muscles to push the shopping cart. Hence, she is applying muscular force on the cart. Q.

Concept note-2: -The man should apply a muscular force opposite to the motion of the cart. Hence, he should apply a force to pull the cart up the slope which would balance out the gravitational force pulling it downwards. Q.

Concept note-3: -Friction is lesser on a cemented road.

Concept note-4: -To move a loaded cart we have to pull it . Hence action involved is as pulling.
