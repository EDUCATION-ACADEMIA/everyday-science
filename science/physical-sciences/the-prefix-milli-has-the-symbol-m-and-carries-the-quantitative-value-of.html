The prefix milli has the symbol “m” and carries the quantitative value of

(A) 1/100, 0.01, 10-2

(B) 1, 000

(C) ** 1/1000, 0.001, 10-3

(D) One million

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In the International System of Units and other metric systems of units, one-thousandth of the unit to which it is prefixed; that is, multiplying the unit to which it is attached by 10-3. Symbol: m. antonym ▲ Example: milligram Antonym: kilo-thousand synonym ▲ Example: millipede Synonym: chilia-

Concept note-2: -Milli (symbol m) is a unit prefix in the metric system denoting a factor of one thousandth (10−3). Proposed in 1793, and adopted in 1795, the prefix comes from the Latin mille, meaning one thousand (the Latin plural is milia).
