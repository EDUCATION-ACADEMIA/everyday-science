How many valence electrons do Group 2 elements have?

(A) 1

(B) ** 2

(C) 7

(D) 8

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -All the elements in Group 2 have two electrons in their valence shells, giving them an oxidation state of +2.

Concept note-2: -Group 2 has 2 valence electrons.

Concept note-3: -Thus, the element in the second group of the periodic table will have an outermost s orbital filled with two electrons of different spins. Hence, the number of valence electrons in group 2 is two.

Concept note-4: -A: Calcium is a group 2 element with two valence electrons.

Concept note-5: -The atomic number of Helium is Z = 2. The electronic configuration is, 2. Therefore, Helium is a noble gas with two valence electrons.
