what equals the size of a liter

(A) 1 yard

(B) 1 small paperclip

(C) ** 2 liter pop

(D) 1 liter pop

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A typical 2 liter soda is equivalent to more than two-thirds of a gallon, or nearly 68 ounces! To put it another way, 2 liters is equal to 67.628045 ounces. This conversion is useful for measuring ingredients in recipes, as well as for monitoring how much liquid you are consuming in a day.

Concept note-2: -The Soda Bottle (2 Liter) has an overall height of 12.4” (31.5 cm) and diameter of 4.33” (11 cm). The Soda Bottle (2 Liter) holds a volume of 67.6 oz (2 L). The Soda Bottle (2 Liter) is most common with soft drinks.

Concept note-3: -The internal diameter of the threaded opening on a two liter launch bottle is 21.0 mm.

Concept note-4: -Answer and Explanation: So, the net volume of the bottle is 2 L which is 2000 mL.

Concept note-5: -A 2-liter coke bottle has a diameter of 110 mm in the middle and length of 40 cm. There is a half-full bottle that is held vertically while the cap is tight.
