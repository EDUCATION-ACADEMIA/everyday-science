How many micrometres are there in a millimetre?

(A) 10

(B) ** 1000

(C) .1

(D) 100

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A Micron contains 0.001 Millimeters or a Millimeter comprises 1000 Microns. 1 Millimeter = 1000 Microns.

Concept note-2: -Solution: We know that one meter is approximately equal to 1000 millimeters.

Concept note-3: -micrometre, also called micron, metric unit of measure for length equal to 0.001 mm, or about 0.000039 inch. Its symbol is m.

Concept note-4: -3. Micrometer A micrometer (also called a micron) is 1000 times smaller than a millimeter. 1 millimeter (mm) = 1000 micrometers (m).
