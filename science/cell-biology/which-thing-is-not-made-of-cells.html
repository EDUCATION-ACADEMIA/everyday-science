Which thing is NOT made of cells?

(A) A dog

(B) A carrot

(C) ** A piece of candy

(D) A piece of wood from a tree

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Thus, option D is correct that is, Water is not made up of cells.

Concept note-2: -Yes, all living things are made up of cells. In 1665, Robert Hooke discovered the existence of cells using a microscope, which further paved the way for the discovery of various other microscopic organisms.

Concept note-3: -Blue Mike and Ikes = mitochondria. Pink Mike and Ikes = smooth endoplasmic reticulum. Air Head Bites = vacuoles. Air Heads Xtremes = rough endoplasmic reticulum. Sour gummy worms = Golgi apparatus. Candy disc sprinkles = ribosomes. Cupcake = nucleus.
