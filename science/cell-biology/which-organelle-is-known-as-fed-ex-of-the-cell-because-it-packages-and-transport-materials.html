Which organelle is known as Fed Ex of the cell because it packages and transport materials?

(A) Vacuole

(B) Mitochondria

(C) Endoplasmic Recticulum

(D) ** Golgi Apparatus

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Golgi apparatus is known as the post office of the cell. It is responsible for packaging, modification and transport of proteins. Linear proteins formed in the endoplasmic reticulum are packaged into vesicles and transported to the golgi apparatus.

Concept note-2: -21 Golgi Body The golgi apparatus changes, sorts, and packages the proteins to be stored in the cell or secreted out of the cell. These organelles convert chemical energy stored in food into compounds that can be used by the cell.

Concept note-3: -The Golgi apparatus, or Golgi complex, functions as a factory in which proteins received from the ER are further processed and sorted for transport to their eventual destinations: lysosomes, the plasma membrane, or secretion. In addition, as noted earlier, glycolipids and sphingomyelin are synthesized within the Golgi.

Concept note-4: -The Golgi apparatus is responsible for transporting, modifying, and packaging proteins and lipids into vesicles for delivery to targeted destinations.

Concept note-5: -Golgi apparatus (aka Golgi body aka Golgi) It is responsible for packing proteins from the rough endoplasmic reticulum into membrane-bound vesicles (tiny compartments of lipid bilayer that store molecules) which then translocate to the cell membrane.
