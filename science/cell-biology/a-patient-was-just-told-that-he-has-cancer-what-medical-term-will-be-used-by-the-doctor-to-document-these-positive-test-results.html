A patient was just told that he has cancer. What medical term will be used by the doctor to document these positive test results?

(A) mitosis

(B) ** diagnosis

(C) prognosis

(D) meiosis

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Tumors may be benign (not cancer) or malignant (cancer). Benign tumors may grow large but do not spread into, or invade, nearby tissues or other parts of the body. Malignant tumors can spread into, or invade, nearby tissues.

Concept note-2: -Multi-cancer detection (MCD) tests are tests that measure biological signals in body fluids that may be shed by cancer cells. These signals are also known as biomarkers or tumor markers. Depending on which signals the MCD test measures, it may detect several different types of cancer.

Concept note-3: -A term used to describe cancer. Malignant cells grow in an uncontrolled way and can invade nearby tissues and spread to other parts of the body through the blood and lymph system.
