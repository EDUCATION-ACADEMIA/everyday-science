Which of these shows the correct order, going from the smallest to the largest?

(A) ** gene, chromosome, nucleus, cell

(B) chromosome, gene, nucleus, cell

(C) nucleus, gene, chromosome, cell

(D) gene, nucleus, chromosome, cell

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Final answer: Genome, Chromosome, Gene, and Nucleotide is the correct order of organization of genetic material from largest to smallest.

Concept note-2: -The order of these structures from smallest to largest is cells, tissues, organs, organ systems.

Concept note-3: -These chromosomes are stored in the nucleus of our cells. So if you put it all together, the order from smallest to largest is option (C), a single gene is smaller than a chromosome is smaller than a nucleus.

Concept note-4: -Answer and Explanation: According to the order form smallest to largest, the order would be: Nucleotide, DNA, genes, chromatin, chromatid, and chromosome.

Concept note-5: -In the human genome, chromosome 1 has the most genes it has 2968 genes. Chromosome 1 is designated for the largest human chromosome. Humans have two copies of chromosome 1, as they do with all of the autosomes, which are the non-sex chromosomes. More items
