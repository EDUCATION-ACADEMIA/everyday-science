Which statement is correct about diffusion?

(A) It’s very rapid over long distances.

(B) ** It’s a passive process which molecules move from a region of higher to lower concentration.

(C) It requires an energy expenditure by the cell.

(D) It’s an active process where molecules move from a region of lower to higher concentration.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Diffusion process does not require energy and thus it is passive transport . Osmosis is a process in which the solvent molecules move from lower solute concentration to higher solute concentration through a semi-permeable membrane. This process does not require energy. Thus, the correct answer is option C.

Concept note-2: -Which of the following statements is correct about passive diffusion? It involves movement of molecules down a concentration gradient and may require a transport protein.

Concept note-3: -It is a passive process in which molecules move from a region of higher concentration to a region of lower concentration.-CORRECT.

Concept note-4: -Diffusion. Diffusion is a passive process of transport. A single substance tends to move from an area of high concentration to an area of low concentration until the concentration is equal across the space. You are familiar with diffusion of substances through the air.

Concept note-5: -Diffusion is a type of passive transport, where molecules move from high concentration to low concentration. This means that the concentration of oxygen in the air must be higher than it is in the blood.
