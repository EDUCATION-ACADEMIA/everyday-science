B1.2.2 Which of the following CANNOT reproduce asexually using mitosis?

(A) ** Humans

(B) Some species of fungus

(C) Some species of plants

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Organisms like bacteria, yeast and hydra reproduce asexually whereas pigeons are birds which undergo sexual reproduction.

Concept note-2: -Asexual Reproduction. When humans reproduce, there are two parents involved. DNA must be passed from both the mother and father to the child. Humans cannot reproduce with just one parent; humans can only reproduce sexually.

Concept note-3: -Humans cannot asexually reproduce because for the offspring to be created, you need a sperm cell and an egg cell. Only females carry and produce egg cells and only males carry and produce sperm cells. Resulting in the inability to asexually reproduce.
