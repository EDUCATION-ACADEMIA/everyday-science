What gland is involved in both digestion and the endocrine system?

(A) Pineal

(B) Pituitary

(C) ** Pancreas

(D) P

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Pancreas. The pancreas is located across the back of the abdomen, behind the stomach. The pancreas plays a role in digestion, as well as hormone production. Hormones produced by the pancreas include insulin and glucagon, which regulate levels of blood sugar.

Concept note-2: -The pancreas has two main functions: an exocrine function that helps in digestion and an endocrine function that regulates blood sugar.

Concept note-3: -The pancreas has dual roles-it is an organ of the digestive system and of the endocrine system. The exocrine pancreas produces enzymes that help to digest food, particularly protein. The endocrine pancreas makes the hormone insulin, which helps to control blood sugar levels.

Concept note-5: -The pancreas has both an endocrine and an exocrine function.
