The water cycle is important

(A) ** True

(B) False

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Water is essential to life on Earth. In its three phases (solid, liquid, and gas), water ties together the major parts of the Earthâ€™s climate system-air, clouds, the ocean, lakes, vegetation, snowpack, and glaciers .

Concept note-2: -The hydrologic cycle is important because it is how water reaches plants, animals and us! Besides providing people, animals and plants with water, it also moves things like nutrients, pathogens and sediment in and out of aquatic ecosystems.

Concept note-3: -Importance of hydrological cycle: As water undergoes infiltration, the ground purifies it of pollutants and contaminants. The water cycle continually feeds freshwater to all life on the planet. Rainfall and surface runoff play important roles in the cycling of various elements, also known as the biogeochemical cycle.

Concept note-4: -Overall, the water cycle process describes how water is balanced in the atmosphere. It also plays an important role in ensuring the availability of water for all living organisms and also it has a great impact on our environment.

Concept note-5: -Water cycle is the process by which water continually changes its form and circulates between oceans, atmosphere and land. The major sources of freshwater are the rivers, ponds, springs and glaciers. The movements that occur in oceans can be broadly categorised as waves, tides and currents.
