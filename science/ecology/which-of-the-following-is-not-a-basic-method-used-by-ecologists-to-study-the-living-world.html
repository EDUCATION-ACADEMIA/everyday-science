Which of the following is NOT a basic method used by ecologists to study the living world?

(A) experimenting

(B) ** animal training

(C) modeling

(D) observing

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Answer and Explanation: The correct choice is A. animal training. Ecologists are the individuals who partake the study of the relationship between living organisms and the environment, that is, ecology.

Concept note-2: -The three main research methods used are observation, modeling, and experimentation.

Concept note-3: -There are three broad approaches to the science of ecology – theoretical ecology, empirical ecology, and applied ecology.
