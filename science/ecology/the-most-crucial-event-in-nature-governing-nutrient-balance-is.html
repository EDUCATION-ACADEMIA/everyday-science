The most crucial event in nature governing nutrient balance is.

(A) Primary production

(B) Secondary production

(C) ** Nutrient cycling

(D) Gross production

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Nutrient cycles restore ecosystems to the equilibrium state, and therefore play an important role in keeping the ecosystem functioning. All organisms, living and non-living depend on one another. Nutrient cycles link living organisms with non-living organisms through the flow of nutrients.

Concept note-2: -Nutrient cycling is a cyclic process that encompasses the movement of nutrients from the physical environment to living organisms and back to the environment. Nutrients are present on the earth where they are recycled, transformed into different forms and reutilized.

Concept note-3: -Without the nutrient cycle, the remains of dead plants and animals would accumulate on the forest floor. And all forest life would collapse because vital compounds would remain tied up in the debris without decomposing it. The 3 main steps for the nutrient cycle are: Plants absorb nutrients from the atmosphere and soil.
