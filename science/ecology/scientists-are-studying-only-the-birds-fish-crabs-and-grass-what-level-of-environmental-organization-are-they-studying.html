Scientists are studying only the birds, fish, crabs, and grass. What level of environmental organization are they studying?

(A) Organism

(B) Population

(C) ** Community

(D) Ecosystem

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Within the discipline of ecology, researchers work at five broad levels, sometimes discretely and sometimes with overlap: organism, population, community, ecosystem, and biosphere. Letâ€™s take a look at each level.

Concept note-2: -What are five levels of organization in an environment? Levels of organization in ecology include the population, community, ecosystem, biome, and biosphere.

Concept note-3: -An ecological community is a group of actually or potentially interacting species living in the same location. Communities are bound together by a shared environment and a network of influence each species has on the other.

Concept note-4: -A community consists of all of the organisms living in a particular area interacting with one another, and an ecosystem consists of all of the organisms living in a particular area interacting with themselves and their environment.
