Autecology deals with ecology of.

(A) ** Single species

(B) Many species

(C) Community

(D) Area

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -autecology, also called Species Ecology, the study of the interactions of an individual organism or a single species with the living and nonliving factors of its environment.

Concept note-2: -Study of a single species and the environmental factors is called autecology. Autecology is also called as Species Ecology. Autecology is experimental and primarily deals with variables that can be easily measured such as light, humidity, and available nutrients.

Concept note-3: -Autecology is the study of individual organisms. Concept note-4: -Autecology is the division of ecology that describes the biological relationship between an individual organism or an individual species and with their environment.

Concept note-5: -So, the correct answer is option A-Ecology of species.
