Immigration is ____

(A) ** The action of coming to live permanently in a foreign country

(B) The act of leaving one’s own country to settle permanently in another

(C) Seasonal movement of animals from one region to another

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Emigrate means to leave one’s country to live in another. Immigrate is to come into another country to live permanently.

Concept note-2: -immigration, process through which individuals become permanent residents or citizens of another country. Historically, the process of immigration has been of great social, economic, and cultural benefit to states.

Concept note-3: -Immigration means the act of coming to your own country. Emigration means the act of leaving your own country. Immigration can be said as in-migration which means a person who has moved to a separate country. Emigration is moving out of one’s own country and settling there permanently.

Concept note-4: -People coming into your country from another country for the purpose of taking up residence is called immigration and the people are called immigrants. The process in which a person leaves their own country in order to settle permanently in another is called emigration.

Concept note-5: -Immigration is the process of moving to a new country or region with the intention of staying and living there. People may choose to immigrate for a variety of reasons, such as employment opportunities, to escape a violent conflict, environmental factors, educational purposes, or to reunite with family.
