A habitat is ____

(A) The way different animals act over time

(B) ** The place where an organism lives

(C) The best place to hide when there is a fire

(D) Living factors in an environment

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A habitat is an environment where an organism lives throughout the year or for shorter periods of time to find a mate. The habitat contains all an animal needs to survive such as food and shelter. A microhabitat is a small area which differs somehow from the surrounding habitat.

Concept note-2: -Habitat is the natural home or environment of an animal, plant or any other organism.

Concept note-3: -The area where a particular organism lives naturally is called its habitat. The five major habitats are – forests, grasslands, deserts, mountains, polar regions and aquatic habitat. Oceans and freshwater together form the aquatic habitat.

Concept note-4: -Q1) What is a habitat? Answer: Solution: The surroundings where plants and animals live is called their habitat. For example, the habitat of a frog is fresh water, while the habitat of a camel is a desert.

Concept note-5: -The environment in which an organism lives is called its habitat.
