Balance this equation, Ca + H2O → CaO + H 2

(A) Ca + 2H2O → CaO + 2H2

(B) 2Ca + H2O → CaO + H2

(C) ** Its balanced as it is

(D) Cannot be balanced

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The balanced chemical equation is as follows: Ca + 2 H 2 O → Ca ( OH ) 2 + H 2 Calcium Water Calcium hydroxide Hydrogen. Q.

Concept note-2: -The balanced equation of calcium oxide with water is written as below: CaO + H2O—Ca(OH)2.

Concept note-3: -The reaction Ca+2H2O⟶Ca(OH)2+H2 is an example of redox reaction.

Concept note-4: -The balanced eqn is : Ca+2H2O→Ca(OH)2+H2.

Concept note-5: -This is the balanced chemical equation as the reaction is already balanced.
