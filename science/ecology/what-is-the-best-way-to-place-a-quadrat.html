Concept note-1: -For instance, always place the quadrat right over the transect point, which is easy to remember although the transect tape might obscure some organisms, or always place the (e.g. bottom left) corner of the quadrat over the transect point.

Concept note-2: -What is the best way to sample sessile organism with quadrats? Randomly place quadrats in as many locations as possible.

Concept note-3: -The usual procedure for randomly positioning quadrats is this: A base line is marked out along the edge of the habitat using a measuring tape. It must extend all the way along the edge of the habitat. Random numbers are obtained using either a table or a random number generator on a calculator.
