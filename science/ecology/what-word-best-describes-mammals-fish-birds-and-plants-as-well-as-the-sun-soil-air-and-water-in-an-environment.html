What word best describes mammals, fish, birds, and plants, as well as the sun, soil, air and water in an environment?

(A) Abiotic

(B) ** Ecosystem

(C) Community

(D) Biosphere

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Biotic factors include plants, animals, and other organisms.

Concept note-2: -what word describes the mammals, fish, birds, and plants that live in an environment? organism, population, community, ecosystem, biosphere.

Concept note-3: -An ecosystem consists of a community of organisms together with their physical environment.

Concept note-4: -Biotic Components of Ecosystem The living components of an ecosystem are called the biotic components. Some of these factors include plants, animals, as well as fungi and bacteria.

Concept note-5: -Forest Ecosystem. Grassland Ecosystem. Tundra Ecosystem. Desert Ecosystem.
