The populations of different organisms living in a defined area are all part of the same

(A) competition

(B) commensalism

(C) ** community

(D) population

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -All organisms living together in an area make up a community. For example, the population of all plant and animal species living in a specific area together constitute a biotic or living community. The members of a community are dependent on each other for food, energy and other needs.

Concept note-2: -So, the correct answer is ‘A community’.

Concept note-3: -Community-a group of different populations that live together in a defined area.

Concept note-4: -Population: A group of organisms of the same species, living in the same area at the same time.
