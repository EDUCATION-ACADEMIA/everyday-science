An idea picture, a system, or a mathematical expression that represents the concept being explained is called a

(A) scientific theory

(B) scientific law

(C) scientific problem

(D) ** scientific model

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Scientific Model. An idea picture, a system, or a mathematical expression that represents the concept being explained. Scientific Theory. An explanation based on many observations during repeated investigations.

Concept note-2: -A scientific model is an idea picture, a system, or a mathematical expression that represents the concept being explained.

Concept note-3: -The 4 types of scientific models are representational, descriptive, spatial and mathematical models.

Concept note-4: -There are three types of models that are generally grouped. They include visual models, mathematical models, and computer models. All three types of models can be used in every branch of science. Each depends largely on the type of scientific inquiry and the form of the data collected.
