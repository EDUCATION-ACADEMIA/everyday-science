Concept note-1: -Specific heat is the amount of energy, measured in joules, needed to raise the temperature of one gram of the substance one Celsius degree. Often applied to metallic elements, specific heat can be used as a basis for comparing how different substances absorb and transfer energy.

Concept note-2: -Specific heat of a substance is the amount of heat to raise the temperature of unit mass of that substance by unit degree centigrade or Kelvin.
