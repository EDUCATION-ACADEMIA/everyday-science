Concept note-1: -What is a Standard Unit of Measurement? The standard unit of measurement is a value that is fixed and cannot be changed. It is needed to have uniformity in measurement. The measurement is measured as feet, inches, and pounds in the United States and meters, centimetres, and kilograms in the metric system.

Concept note-2: -Measurement is the comparison of an unknown quantity with a known standard quantity (constant quantity) or unit.

Concept note-3: -Standard units of measuring length Some examples of measurement of length using standard units are centimeters, meters, kilometers, inches, feet, yards, etc.
