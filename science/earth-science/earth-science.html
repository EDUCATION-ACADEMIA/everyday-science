Earth Science

(A) Study of people

(B) ** Study of the earth’s land, water, air and outer space

(C) Study of plants and animals

(D) Study of bones

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Physical geography is the study of Earth’s seasons, climate, atmosphere, soil, streams, landforms, and oceans.

Concept note-2: -Earth sciences are the fields of study concerned with the solid Earth, its waters, and the air that envelops it. They include the geologic, hydrologic, and atmospheric sciences with the broad aim of understanding Earth’s present features and past evolution and using this knowledge to benefit humankind.

Concept note-3: -Geoscience (also called Earth Science) is the study of Earth. Geoscience includes so much more than rocks and volcanoes, it studies the processes that form and shape Earth’s surface, the natural resources we use, and how water and ecosystems are interconnected.

Concept note-4: -The four basic areas of Earth Science study are: geology, meteorology, oceanography and astronomy. Geology is the primary Earth science.

Concept note-5: -Earth and Space Sciences seeks to further our understanding of the Earth, the solar system, and their histories. The scope extends from the center of Earth to the rim of the solar system, and activities cut across traditional disciplines of physics, chemistry, biology, geology, and mathematics.
