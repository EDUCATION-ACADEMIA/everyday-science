Earth is tilted on its ____, which is an imaginary line that runs through Earth.

(A) orbit

(B) day

(C) tilt

(D) ** axis

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The imaginary line drawn straight through Earth connecting the North Pole to the South Pole is Earth’s axis of rotation. Q. The imaginary line around an object in which it rotates is called its axis of rotation.

Concept note-2: -The imaginary line in a tilted manner on earth is called axis. Two points on the globe through which this line passes are two poles – North Pole and South Pole. Was this answer helpful?

Concept note-3: -The axis of the Earth is an imaginary line which joins north pole to south pole. It makes an angle of 66.5° with its orbital plane. Q. Rotation is the movement of the Earth around an imaginary axis passing through its poles.

Concept note-4: -This imaginary line that passes through the North Pole and South Pole is known as the Axis.

Concept note-5: -Earth’s Axis Is Imaginary, an axis refers to the imaginary line that an object, usually a planet, rotates around. Earth’s rotational axis is an imaginary straight line that runs through the North and South Pole.
