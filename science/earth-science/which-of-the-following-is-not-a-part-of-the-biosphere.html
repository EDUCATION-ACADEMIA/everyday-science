Which of the following is not a part of the Biosphere?

(A) plants and animals

(B) dead organic matter produced by different organisms

(C) different bacteria and microorganisms.

(D) ** none of the above.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The lithosphere is known as the terrestrial component of the biosphere. For instance, it contains solid landmasses like our continents and islands. The part that does not sustain any life, and therefore, not a part of the biosphere, is its lower mantle and core.

Concept note-2: -The biosphere is the region of the Earth where life can be found, including the soil, water, and air. The lithosphere, hydrosphere, and atmosphere are the names for these three components. The lithosphere is the landmass of the Earth, except for the mantle and core, which are inhospitable to life.

Concept note-3: -The five systems of Earth (geosphere, biosphere, cryosphere, hydrosphere, and atmosphere) interact to produce the environments we are familiar with.

Concept note-4: -The biosphere includes the outer region of the earth (the lithosphere) and the lower region of the atmosphere (the troposphere). It also includes the hydrosphere, the region of lakes, oceans, streams, ice and clouds comprising the earthâ€™s water resources.
