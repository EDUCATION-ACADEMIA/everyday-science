The water found in the atmosphere in the form of a gas is called what?

(A) Precipitation

(B) Water sprite

(C) ** Water vapor

(D) Clouds

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Water vapor, water vapour or aqueous vapor is the gaseous phase of water. It is one state of water within the hydrosphere. Water vapor can be produced from the evaporation or boiling of liquid water or from the sublimation of ice. Water vapor is transparent, like most constituents of the atmosphere.

Concept note-2: -humidity, the amount of water vapour in the air. It is the most variable characteristic of the atmosphere and constitutes a major factor in climate and weather.

Concept note-3: -Although invisible, water vapour is one component of the wet steam released by a boiling kettle, the visible portion is microscopic droplets of water suspended in the air. Water vapour is water in gaseous instead of liquid form. It can be formed either through a process of evaporation or sublimation.
