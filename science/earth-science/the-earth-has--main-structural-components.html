The Earth has ____ main structural components.

(A) 2

(B) ** 4

(C) 3

(D) 6

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The structure of the earth is divided into four major components: the crust, the mantle, the outer core, and the inner core . Each layer has a unique chemical composition, physical state, and can impact life on Earth’s surface.

Concept note-2: -Inner Core. The inner core is the deepest layer on Earth.

Concept note-3: -The earth is made up of three different layers: the crust, the mantle and the core. This is the outside layer of the earth and is made of solid rock, mostly basalt and granite. There are two types of crust; oceanic and continental.

Concept note-4: -The earth is made up of three concentric layers-crust, mantle and core. The uppermost layer over the earth’s surface is called the crust. It is about 35 km thick on the continental masses and only 5 km thick on the ocean floor. It is made up of silica and alumina and thus called sial.

Concept note-5: -Core, mantle, and crust are divisions based on composition. The crust makes up less than 1 percent of Earth by mass, consisting of oceanic crust and continental crust is often more felsic rock. The mantle is hot and represents about 68 percent of Earth’s mass. Finally, the core is mostly iron metal.
