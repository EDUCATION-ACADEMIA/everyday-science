What does ROTATION mean?

(A) ** spinning

(B) moving around

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Some Definitions Spin motion: Motion of an object as it rotates around an axis through its center of mass. Rotational motion: Motion around an axis of rotation. Both orbital and spin motion are examples of rotational motion.

Concept note-2: -Rotation describes the circular motion of an object around its center. There are different ways things can rotate. Rotation of the Earthre. A very familiar kind of rotation is when a spherical, three-dimensional object turns around an invisible line inside its center. This center is called an axis.

Concept note-3: -: the act or an instance of rotating something. : one complete turn : the angular displacement required to return a rotating body or figure to its original orientation. : return or succession in a series. rotation of the seasons. : crop rotation.

Concept note-4: -The Earth spins because it formed in the accretion disk of a cloud of hydrogen that collapsed down from mutual gravity and needed to conserve its angular momentum. It continues to spin because of inertia.
