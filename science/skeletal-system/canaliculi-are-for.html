Canaliculi are for

(A) providing protection

(B) housing osteocytes

(C) ** providing nourishment

(D) holding bone together

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -It helps the bone cells to receive food and oxygen and also to eliminate waste materials.

Concept note-2: -Blood and Nerve Supply The osteocytes in spongy bone are nourished by blood vessels of the periosteum that penetrate spongy bone and blood that circulates in the marrow cavities. As the blood passes through the marrow cavities, it is collected by veins, which then pass out of the bone through the foramina.

Concept note-3: -Bone canaliculi are microscopic canals between the lacunae of ossified bone. The radiating processes of the osteocytes (called filopodia) project into these canals. These cytoplasmic processes are joined together by gap junctions.
