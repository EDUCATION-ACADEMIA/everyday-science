(D) Telescope

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A quality stethoscope. An appropriately sized blood pressure cuff. A blood pressure measurement instrument such as an aneroid or mercury column sphygmomanometer or an automated device with a manual inflate mode. 27-Jul-2009

Concept note-2: -To measure blood pressure, your doctor uses an instrument call a sphygmomanometer, which is more often referred to as a blood pressure cuff. The cuff is wrapped around your upper arm and inflated to stop the flow of blood in your artery.

Pump up the cuff slowly and listen for when the pulse disappears. This is an indication to stop inflating the cuff.

Concept note-4: -The bell of the stethoscope is suggested because it is used for low-pitched sounds like blood pressure.
