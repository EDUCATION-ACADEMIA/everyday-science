The time period that greatly improved the quality of life for humans but also uses triggered the use of nonrenewable resources at a level that is not sustainable.

(A) ** Industrial Revolution

(B) Agricultural Revolution

(C) Hunter-Gatherer Period

(D) The Great Depression

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Therefore, sustainable development aims at sustaining the present level of resources and providing the present generation with good quality life where good quality of life refers to standard living with all necessary and basic needs.

Concept note-2: -Sustainable development means to protect, preserve and conserve the natural environment for the future needs. Besides, sustainable development will only succeed if the environmental components achieve their stability and the society experience good quality of life upon the natural environment.

Concept note-3: -Taking these three pillars of sustainability further if we only achieve two out of three pillars then we end up with: Social + Economic Sustainability = Equitable. Social + Environmental Sustainability = Bearable.
