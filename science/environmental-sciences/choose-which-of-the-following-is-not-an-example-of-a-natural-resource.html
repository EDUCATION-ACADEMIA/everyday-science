Choose which of the following is NOT an example of a natural resource.

(A) coal

(B) sunlight

(C) timber (trees)

(D) ** plastic

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Minerals, water and soil are natural resources but plastic is a man-made material. So, plastic is not a natural resource.

Concept note-2: -Plastic is a man-made resource, which is prepared by the human.

Concept note-3: -Plastics are made from natural materials such as cellulose, coal, natural gas, salt and crude oil through a polymerisation or polycondensation process. Plastics are derived from natural, organic materials such as cellulose, coal, natural gas, salt and, of course, crude oil.

Concept note-4: -So, electricity is not a natural resource.

Concept note-5: -Petrol, metals, soil, sand, wind, water and everything in between are natural resources. Manufactured items such as plastic, sheet metal, fabrics, microchips, electricity and concrete are not natural resources, but are most definitely derived from natural resources.
