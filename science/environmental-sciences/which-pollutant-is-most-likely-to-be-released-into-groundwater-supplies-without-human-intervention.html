Concept note-2: -Septic tanks are often used in rural areas, campgrounds, and picnic areas in place of sewer systems to treat human waste and separate solids and liquids in wastewater. Concept note-3: -Researchers found high levels of vitellogenin-a protein involved in producing egg yolk-in many smallmouth bass. In male fish, the gene that tells the body to produce vitellogenin is usually “turned off, ” explains Iwanowicz. That gene only “switches on” in the presence of estrogen, a female sex hormone.
