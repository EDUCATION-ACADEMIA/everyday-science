Identify the following:We should recognize our moral duty to protect the welfare not only human beings, but also of other non-human parts of this system.

(A) ** The Ethical Values for Pollution Control

(B) Ethical Values for Conserving depletable resources

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Bioethics, which promotes a set of principles to guide the interaction between the human race and living things-both fellow human beings and other forms of life-must now more than ever be linked to environmental protection.

Concept note-2: -Based on this argument, we have a moral responsibility to steward towards environmental protection. Therefore, human beings have a moral obligation to conserve the environment in order to make certain our survival and the survival of other species.

Concept note-3: -Instead, they advocate what may be called enlightened anthropocentrism (or, perhaps more appropriately called, prudential anthropocentrism). Briefly, this is the view that all the moral duties we have towards the environment are derived from our direct duties to its human inhabitants.

Concept note-4: -Environmental ethics is the discipline in philosophy that studies the moral relationship of human beings to, and also the value and moral status of, the environment and its nonhuman contents.
