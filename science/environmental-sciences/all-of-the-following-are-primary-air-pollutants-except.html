All of the following are primary air pollutants except

(A) carbon monoxide

(B) ** nitric acid

(C) sulfur oxides.

(D) VOCs.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Different types of secondary pollutants include: Ozone (O3) Sulfuric acid and nitric acid (component of acid rain)

Concept note-2: -Solution(By Examveda Team) All are primary pollutants except Peroxyacetyl Nitrate (PAN).

Concept note-3: -Primary air pollutants: Pollutants that are formed and emitted directly from particular sources. Examples are particulates, carbon monoxide, nitrogen oxide, and sulfur oxide.

Concept note-4: -Nitric acid (HNO3) is a secondary pollutant formed in the troposphere primarily from the oxidation of NOx (NOx = NO + NO2) emitted from vehicle tailpipes and other combustion sources (for example, power generation).
