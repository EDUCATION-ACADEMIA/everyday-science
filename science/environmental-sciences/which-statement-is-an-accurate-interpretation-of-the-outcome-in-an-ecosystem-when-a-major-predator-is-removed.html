Which statement is an accurate interpretation of the outcome in an ecosystem when a major predator is removed?

(A) The remaining community adjusts and quickly becomes stable.

(B) The diversity of the ecosystem actually increases.

(C) ** The diversity of the ecosystem decreases and the prey population increases.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In predation, one organism kills and consumes another. Predation provides energy to prolong the life and promote the reproduction of the organism that does the killing, the predator, to the detriment of the organism being consumed, the prey. Predation influences organisms at two ecological levels.

Concept note-2: -Population Explosions The most obvious result of the removal of the top predators in an ecosystem is a population explosion in the prey species. Predators keep herbivore populations in check. The reverse is also true, of courseâ€“predator populations are limited by the availability of prey.

Concept note-4: -As predator populations increase, they put greater strain on the prey populations and act as a top-down control, pushing them toward a state of decline. Thus both availability of resources and predation pressure affect the size of prey populations.
