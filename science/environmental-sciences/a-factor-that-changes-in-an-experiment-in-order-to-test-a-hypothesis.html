A factor that changes in an experiment in order to test a hypothesis

(A) Knowledge

(B) Control Group

(C) Observation

(D) ** Variable

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Answer and Explanation: When conducting an experiment to test a hypothesis, the changeable factor that is studied is called the variable.

Concept note-2: -Manipulated variable: The factor in an experiment that is deliberately changed to test the hypothesis.

Concept note-3: -The independent variable is the one that is changed by the scientist. To insure a fair test, a good experiment has only ONE independent variable. As the scientist changes the independent variable, he or she records the data that they collect.

Concept note-4: -An independent variable is the variable you manipulate, control, or vary in an experimental study to explore its effects.

Concept note-5: -Experimental Variables A manipulated variable is a variable that is changed by the researcher. A manipulated variable is also called an independent variable.
