An example of an inexhaustible resource would be:

(A) ** Sunlight

(B) Coal

(C) Iron Ore

(D) Trees

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Inexhaustible sources of energy are those that will not get exhausted in the future. They are in unlimited supply. Sunlight and air are examples of inexhaustible natural resources. They will not get depleted with use.

Concept note-2: -Sunlight and air are inexhaustible natural resources because they are in unlimited quantities and cannot be exhausted by any means.

Concept note-3: -Inexhaustible resources: Wind, sunlight and water.

Concept note-4: -Answer: Sunlight is present in unlimited and they will not get exhausted by any means. Hence Sunlight is inexhaustible natural resources.

Concept note-5: -Sunlight is a renewable resource, and its most direct use is achieved by capturing the sun’s energy. A variety of solar energy technologies are used to convert the sun’s energy and light into heat: illumination, hot water, electricity and (paradoxically) cooling systems for businesses and industry.
