Concept note-1: -Africa: fastest growing continent More than half of global population growth between now and 2050 is expected to occur in Africa. Africa has the highest rate of population growth among major areas. The population of sub-Saharan Africa is projected to double by 2050.

Concept note-2: -Figure 1.2 shows typical age structures for countries that have different rates of growth. Countries that have high rates of growth usually have more young people than older people. Countries that have slow growth or no growth usually have an even distribution of ages.
