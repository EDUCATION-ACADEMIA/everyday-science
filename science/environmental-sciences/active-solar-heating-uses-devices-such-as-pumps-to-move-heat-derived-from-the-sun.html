Active solar heating uses devices such as pumps to move heat derived from the sun.

(A) ** True

(B) False

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In active solar heating applications, heat from the sun is collected, stored and used primarily for domestic hot water heating but also can be used for space heating. The reason the system is called active is because pumps and fans are used to transfer the captured heat to an area where it can be stored or used.

Concept note-2: -Passive solar systems rely on the structure of the building to collect heat. This could be in the form of a tilt or a roof orientation that allows for higher solar irradiance. On the contrary, active solar heating systems rely on heat pumps that transfer the collected heat from the solar collectors to the building.

Concept note-3: -On the other hand, active solar heating convert heat from the sun into an alternate source, like electricity or hot water. Photovoltaic solar panels that are used to run an electric heater would be one example of active solar heating.
