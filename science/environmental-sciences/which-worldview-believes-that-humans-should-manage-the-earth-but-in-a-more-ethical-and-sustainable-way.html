Which worldview believes that Humans should manage the Earth, but in a more ethical and sustainable way?

(A) Planetary management

(B) Gross domestic product

(C) ** Stewardship

(D) Environmental wisdom

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -One such worldview held by many people is the planetary management world-view. Figure 25-2 (left) summarizes the major beliefs of this worldview. According to this view, humans are the planet’s most important and dominant species, and we can and should manage the earth mostly for our own benefit.

Concept note-2: -Biocentric worldviews are life-focused and view the environment from a harmonious, protective perspective. These worldviews are built on the fundamental belief that humans are one with nature and have an ethical responsibility to protect and preserve the planet.

Concept note-3: -A life-centered environmental worldview sees all living things as having value regardless of their use to humans and that we have a responsibility to avoid hastening any extinctions through our activities.

Concept note-4: -An individual’s environmental ethics are what they believe is right or wrong about our behavior toward the environment. There are three major environmental worldviews. They are the “planetary management” view, the “stewardship” view, and the “environmental wisdom” view.
