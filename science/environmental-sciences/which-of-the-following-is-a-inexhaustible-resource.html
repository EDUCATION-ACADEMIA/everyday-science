Which of the following is a inexhaustible resource?

(A) ** wind

(B) coal

(C) oil

(D) gas

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Wind is considered as renewable and inexhaustible source of energy because it has an endless supply.

Concept note-2: -Wind energy is a source of renewable energy. It does not contaminate, it is inexhaustible and reduces the use of fossil fuels, which are the origin of greenhouse gasses that cause global warming.

Concept note-3: -Air is a natural resource and cannot be exhausted by human activities. It is known as an inexhaustible natural resource.

Concept note-4: -An inexhaustible resource is a resource that never runs out or gets depleted. Some of such resources include wind, sun, solar energy, tides, and geothermal energy.

Concept note-5: -An inexhaustible resource is one that can be replenished, while an exhaustible resource cannot be replenished. Wind, sunlight and water are inexhaustible resources while petroleum, coal, natural gas, forests and minerals are exhaustible resources.
