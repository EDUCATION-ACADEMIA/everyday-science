Which of the following is NOT a mechanism of heat energy transfer through or within Earth’s atmosphere?

(A) Radiation

(B) ** Condensation

(C) Convection

(D) Conduction

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution So Radiation, Conduction and Convection are 3 basic types of heat transfer and induction is not a mode of heat transfer.

Concept note-2: -Energy is transferred between the Earth’s surface and the atmosphere in a variety of ways, including radiation, conduction, and convection. Conduction is one of the three main ways that heat energy moves from place to place. The other two ways heat moves around are radiation and convection.

Concept note-3: -Turns out there are four methods for body heat loss: radiation, conduction, convection and evaporation.

Concept note-4: -radiation. conduction. convection.
