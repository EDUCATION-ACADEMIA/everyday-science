Botany is the scientific study of.

(A) Cells

(B) Organisms

(C) Human bodies

(D) ** Plants

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Botany is the scientific study of plants-how plants function, what they look like, how they are related to each other, where they grow, how people make use of plants, and how plants evolved.

Concept note-2: -Plant science encompasses ‘Botany’ from the Greek word (botanē) meaning pasture or fodder, in that it involves the observation of plant growth and characteristics and investigation into the relationship between plants and their environment.

Concept note-3: -The experts who study plants are called Botanists.

Concept note-4: -The science that studies plants is called Botany. All natural sciences, including botany, aim to understand how the natural world functions. To be able to do this we need to make observations, run experiments, generate and test hypotheses, record data, and report results.

Concept note-5: -Difference Between Botany and Zoology Botany is the study of plants. Zoology is the study of animals.
