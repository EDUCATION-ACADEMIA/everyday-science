The science which deals with influence of stars on human fate and destiny is called.

(A) Astronomy

(B) Anatomy

(C) ** Astrology

(D) Nature

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -astrology, type of divination that involves the forecasting of earthly and human events through the observation and interpretation of the fixed stars, the Sun, the Moon, and the planets.

Concept note-2: -Astrology is generally defined as the belief that astronomical phenomena, like the stars overhead when you were born or the fact that Mercury is in retrograde, have the power to influence the daily events in our lives and our personality traits.

Concept note-3: -Astrology (from the Greek astron, “stars", “stars", “celestial bodies", and logos, “word", “study") is a pseudoscience according to which the relative positions of celestial bodies could provide information about personality, human relations, and other matters related to human life.

Concept note-4: -Astronomers base their studies on research and observation. Astrology, on the other hand, is the belief that the positioning of the stars and planets affect the way events occur on earth.

Concept note-5: -Astrology is the scientific study of stars and other celestial bodies.
