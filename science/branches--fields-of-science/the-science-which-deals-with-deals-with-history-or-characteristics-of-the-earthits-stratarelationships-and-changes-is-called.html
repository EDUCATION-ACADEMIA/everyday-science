Concept note-1: -Geology describes the structure of the Earth on and beneath its surface, and the processes that have shaped that structure. Geologists study the mineralogical composition of rocks in order to get insight into their history of formation.

Concept note-2: -Stratigraphy is a branch of Geology and the Earth Sciences that deals with the arrangement and succession of strata, or layers, as well as the origin, composition and distribution of these geological strata. The study of archaeological and natural stratification therefore involves the assessment of TIME and SPACE.

Concept note-3: -Geology : is the scientific study of the earth-the material of which it is made, the processes that act on these materials, the products formed, and the history of the planet and its life forms since origin.
