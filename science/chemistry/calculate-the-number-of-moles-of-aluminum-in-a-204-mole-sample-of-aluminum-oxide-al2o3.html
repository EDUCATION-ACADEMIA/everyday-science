Calculate the number of moles of Aluminum in a 2.04 mole sample of aluminum oxide, Al2O3.

(A) 2.04 moles

(B) ** 4.08 moles

(C) 6.12 moles

(D) 1.02 moles

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The mole ratio of Al to Al2O3 is 4:2. Use this mole ratio for the stoichiometry conversion.

Concept note-2: -1 Expert Answer 4:3 is the mole ratio of aluminum to oxygen gas.

Concept note-3: -Molecular weight of Al2O3=102g. Weight of Al in Al2O3=2×27=54g. Amount of Al in 102g of Al2O3=54g. Amount of Al in 9000g of Al2O3=10254×9000=4764. 7g.
