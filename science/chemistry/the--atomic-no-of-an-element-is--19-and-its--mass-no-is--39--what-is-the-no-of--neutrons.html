The atomic no . of an element is 19 and its mass no . is 39 . What is the no. of neutrons ?

(A) 19

(B) 39

(C) 58

(D) ** 20

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Hence, no. of neutrons = mass number-number of protons =39−19=20.

Concept note-2: -The element with the atomic number 19 is a metal called potassium. Its atomic symbol is K.

Concept note-3: -The element potassium’s atomic number is 19. Its relative atomic mass is 39.098.

Concept note-4: -Therefore, the number of neutrons in an atom having mass number 39 and atomic number 19 is 20.
