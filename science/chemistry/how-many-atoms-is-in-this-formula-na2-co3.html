How many atoms is in this formula? Na2 CO3

(A) 3

(B) 4

(C) 5

(D) ** 6

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Na2CO3 denotes 1 molecule of sodium carbonate, which is made up of 2 atoms of sodium, 1 atom of carbon and 3 atoms of oxygen.

Concept note-2: -Conclusion: NaHCO3 has 6 atoms.

Concept note-3: -Sodium bicarbonate (NaHCO3), more commonly known as baking soda, is a chemical compound. It is composed of three atoms of oxygen (O) and one atom each of sodium (Na), hydrogen (H), and carbon (C).

Concept note-4: -Its chemical formula is NaHCO3. Its formula consists of one sodium (Na) atom, one hydrogen (H) atom, one carbon (C) atom and three oxygen (O) atoms.
