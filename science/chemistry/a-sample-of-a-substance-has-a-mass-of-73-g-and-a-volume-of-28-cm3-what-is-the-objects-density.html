A sample of a substance has a mass of 7.3 g and a volume of 2.8 cm3. What is the objects density?

(A) ** 2.61 g/cm3

(B) 10.1 g/cm3

(C) 0.38 g/cm3

(D) 20.44 g/cm3

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The density of the given substance is 2.61 g/cm³.

Concept note-2: -or, Volume = mass/density = 64 g/0.8g/cm3 = 80cm3.

Concept note-3: -✒ Therefore, Density of the substance = 2.7 g/cm³.

Concept note-4: -Iron has a density of 7.9 g/cm^3.

Concept note-5: -3.8 ml is the volume of an object with 3.1 g/ml density and 12 g mass.
