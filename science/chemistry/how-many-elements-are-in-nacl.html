How many elements are in NaCl?

(A) ** 2

(B) 3

(C) 1

(D) 4

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Sodium Chloride or NaCl is made up of two elements, sodium (or Na) and chlorine (or Cl). A molecule of sodium chloride, NaCl, consists of one atom each of sodium and chlorine. Hence, each molecule of NaCl has 2 atoms total."

Concept note-2: -Chemically, table salt consists of two elements, sodium (Na) and chloride (Cl).

Concept note-3: -Chemical abbreviation for sodium chloride (table salt).

Concept note-4: -Sodium chloride (chemical formula NaCl), known as table salt, rock salt, sea salt and the mineral halite, is an ionic compound consisting of cube-shaped crystals composed of the elements sodium and chlorine .

Concept note-5: -2.0 moles Na x 6.022⋅1023g1mol=1.20⋅1024 atoms of Na.
