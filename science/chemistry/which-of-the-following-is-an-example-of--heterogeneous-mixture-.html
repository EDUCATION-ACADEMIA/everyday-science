Which of the following is an example of Heterogeneous Mixture .

(A) ** oil & water

(B) salt & water

(C) sugar & water

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Sand in water, Oil in water are examples of heterogeneous mixtures.

Concept note-2: -Hence, oil in water is a heterogeneous mixture.

Concept note-3: -When oil and water are combined, they do not mix evenly, but instead form two separate layers. So, it is a heterogeneous mixture. Homogenous mixtures have uniform composition and they do not separate out into different layers. Mixtures are not pure substances.

Concept note-4: -A mixture of ethyl alcohol and water is homogeneous while that of oil and water is heterogeneous.

Concept note-5: -Concrete is a heterogeneous mixture of an aggregate: cement, and water. Sugar and sand form a heterogeneous mixture. Ice cubes in cola form a heterogeneous mixture.
