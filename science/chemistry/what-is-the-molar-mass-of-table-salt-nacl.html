What is the molar mass of table salt (NaCl)?

(A) 116.886 g/mol

(B) 35.453 g/mol

(C) 22.990 g/mol

(D) ** 58.443 g/mol

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -One mol of NaCl (6.02 x1023 formulas) has a mass of 58.44 g.

Concept note-2: -Molar Mass of a Compound This means that the molar mass of NaCl is 58.44 g and it will contain 6.022 × 1023 formula units of NaCl, or one mole of NaCl.

Concept note-3: -Since 58.5 g of sodium chloride contains 23 g of sodium, ⟹11.7 g of sodium chloride contains 2353.5×11.7 = 4.6 g of sodium.

Concept note-4: -The mass of sodium in 58.5g of NaCl is 23 g.
