Calculate the formula mass of KCl . (Atomic mass of K = 39 , Cl = 35 . 5 )

(A) ** 74.5

(B) 4.5

(C) 90

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -(1atom×39. 0983 Potassium)+(1atom×35. 453 Chlorine) = (74.5513 g/mol) which is the molar mass for KCl.

Concept note-2: -The atomic mass of KCl is 74.6 u, and its molar mass is 74.6 g/mol .

Concept note-3: -Potassium chloride (KCl, or potassium salt) is a metal halide salt composed of potassium and chlorine.
