Which reagent is needed to change CH3CH=CH2 to CH3CHClCH3

(A) Cl2 (aq)

(B) SOCl2

(C) PCl3

(D) ** HCl

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The reagent used will be Mo2O3.

Concept note-2: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Lindlar catalyst.

Concept note-3: -The longest chain of the compound contains 4 carbon atoms, therefore, the root name of this compound is butane.
