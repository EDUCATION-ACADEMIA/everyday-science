Potassium permanganate symbol?

(A) ** KMnO4

(B) N2O

(C) CuS

(D) FeS

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Potassium permanganate is used in water treatment to remove iron and hydrogen sulfide. It is used to keep water free from bacteria. It is also used as an oxidizing agent in organic reactions.

Concept note-2: -Potassium Permanganate Structure – KMnO4 The oxidation state of manganese in this salt is +7.

Concept note-3: -Its chemical formula is KMnO4. It contains potassium and permanganate ions. The manganese is in the +7 oxidation state. It is also known as permanganate of potash and Condy’s crystals.
