Who is someone who studies how to use plants for food and other products?

(A) Botanist

(B) Photosynthesis

(C) ** Horticulturist

(D) Xylem

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A person who studies and practices horticulture is called a Horticulturist. Horticultural science includes the research, study and practice of plant cultivation, plant propagation, plant breeding, production of crops and plant physiology.

Concept note-2: -A horticulturalist is a person who grows flowers, fruit, and vegetables, especially as their job.

Concept note-3: -Botanists interested in ecology study interactions of plants with other organisms and the environment. Other field botanists search to find new species or do experiments to discover how plants grow under different conditions. Some botanists study the structure of plants.

Concept note-4: -Simply put, a horticulturist uses skills and knowledge to cultivate and study different types of plants. This includes vegetables, flowers, grass, seeds, algae and everything in between! Horticulture also includes working in areas like landscape design and plant conservation among others.
