Which of the following plants have seeds

(A) ** Pine Tree

(B) Horsetail

(C) ** Rose

(D) Fern

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Jamun, banyan, apple, coconut and neem are some of the plants that reproduce by means of seeds.

Concept note-2: -On average, there are seven seeds per rose hip (with a range of 1 to 22). Thus, each cane can potentially produce up to 17, 500 seeds (Amrine, 1991).

Concept note-3: -Rose is grown by replanting part of a stem of the rose bush.

Concept note-4: -Once pollen gets to the ovary within the flower, the ovary develops into a fruit. The ovules inside the ovary develop into seeds inside of this fruit.
