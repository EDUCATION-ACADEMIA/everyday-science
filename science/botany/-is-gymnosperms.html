____ is gymnosperms

(A) ** Cycas

(B) Xylaria

(C) Cassia

(D) Coconut

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Is Cycas a flowering plant? Cycas is a gymnosperm that produces naked seeds, but does not produce flowers and fruits.

Concept note-2: -It possesses a naked seed, Cycas belongs to the gymnosperm order Cycadales. It is not contained within a fruit. Because there is no double fertilization, the endosperm produced is haploid rather than triploid. As ovules (or seeds) are created inside the fruit, it is not considered angiosperm.

Concept note-3: -Cycas, Pinus and Ginkgo are examples of Gymnosperm.

Concept note-4: -In Cycas, the seed does not occur inside a fruit but lies naked or exposed on the surface of megasporophyll. Before fertilization and the formation of the seed, the ovule is naked. This is a characteristic of Gymnosperms. Hence Cycas is a gymnosperm.
