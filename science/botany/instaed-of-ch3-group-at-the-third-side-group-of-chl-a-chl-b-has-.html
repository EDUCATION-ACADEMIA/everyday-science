Instaed of-CH3 group at the third side group of chl a, chl b has ____

(A) -COOH group

(B) -CO group

(C) ** -CHO group

(D) -OH group

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Chl a has a methyl group (-CH3) and Chl b has an aldehyde group (-CHO) in its structure.

Concept note-2: -Chlorophyll a contains methyl group (-CH 3 ) and chlorophyll b contains aldehydic group (-CHO) instead. Chlorophyll is a green pigment found in plants.

Concept note-3: -The two chlorophylls differ only at position 3 (ring B): chlorophyll a contains a methyl group at this position, whereas chlorophyll b contains an aldehyde group (1).

Concept note-4: -There are four types of chlorophyll: chlorophyll a, found in all higher plants, algae and cyanobacteria; chlorophyll b, found in higher plants and green algae; chlorophyll c, found in diatoms, dinoflagellates and brown algae; and chlorophyll d, found only in red algae.
