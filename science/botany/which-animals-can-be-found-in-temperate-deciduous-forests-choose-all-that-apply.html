Which animals can be found in temperate deciduous forests? (Choose all that apply.)

(A) ** foxes

(B) ** birds

(C) elephants

(D) alligators

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Fauna Of Temperate Deciduous Forest Common birds found in this biome include robins, woodpeckers, jays, hawks, owls, cardinals, turkeys, and eagles.

Concept note-2: -Insects, spiders, slugs, frogs, turtles and salamanders are common. In North America, birds like broad-winged hawks, cardinals, snowy owls, and pileated woodpeckers are found in this biome. Mammals in North American temperate deciduous forests include white-tailed deer, raccoons, opossums, porcupines and red foxes.

Concept note-3: -In India, Lion, tiger, pig, deer, and elephant are some of the animals that make these forests their homes. Outside India, in the United States, mammals that are generally found in a deciduous forest include bears, raccoons, squirrels, skunks, wood mice and even deer.

Concept note-4: -The flora in temperate evergreen forests consists of small leaved coniferous trees, small leaved deciduous trees, and conifers and the fauna in the forests include moose, reindeers, elk, caribou, racoons, eagles, owls.
