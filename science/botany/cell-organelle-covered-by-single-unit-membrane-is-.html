Cell organelle covered by single unit membrane is ____

(A) Glyoxisome

(B) Lysosome

(C) Peroxisomes

(D) ** All of these.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A single unit membrane organelle is lysosomes. A lysosome is a membrane-bound cell organelle found in most animal cells.

Concept note-2: -Final answer: Vacuole and Lysosomes are bound by a single membrane.

Concept note-3: -The nuclear membrane is a double layer that encloses the cell’s nucleus, where the chromosomes reside. The nuclear membrane serves to separate the chromosomes from the cell’s cytoplasm and other contents.

Concept note-4: -An organelle (think of it as a cell’s internal organ) is a membrane bound structure found within a cell. Just like cells have membranes to hold everything in, these mini-organs are also bound in a double layer of phospholipids to insulate their little compartments within the larger cells.
