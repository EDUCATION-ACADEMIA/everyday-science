Most seeds have the same testa.

(A) True

(B) ** False

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Hence, the given statement is ‘False’.

Concept note-2: -Most plants produce seeds of some kind, but not all seeds are alike. Nature not only has given them different sizes, shapes, and colors, but also has packaged them in any unique ways.

Concept note-3: -(a) In every seed, Cotyledons are present. These are produced with the endosperm within the seed. These are plants categorised on the basis of the presence of cotyledons. However, it is true that there are no cotyledons at all in the seedlings of some flowering plants.

Concept note-4: -The given statement is false. The leaves, roots, stems, fruits and seeds of various plants can all act as sources of food.
