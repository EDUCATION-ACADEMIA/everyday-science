The HYPOTHESIS is:

(A) aspect or factor that is changed in an experiment.

(B) ** tentative, unproven explanation for something that has been observed

(C) group of generalizations that help us understand something

(D) a useful generalization formed through the accumulation of data.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -scientific hypothesis, an idea that proposes a tentative explanation about a phenomenon or a narrow set of phenomena observed in the natural world.

Concept note-2: -A hypothesis is a tentative explanation for an observation, phenomenon, or scientific problem that can be tested by further investigation.

Concept note-3: -A hypothesis is a tentative statement that proposes a possible explanation to some phenomenon or event. A useful hypothesis is a testable statement, which may include a prediction. A hypothesis should not be confused with a theory. Theories are general explanations based on a large amount of data.

Concept note-4: -A hypothesis (plural: hypotheses), in a scientific context, is a testable statement about the relationship between two or more variables or a proposed explanation for some observed phenomenon.

Concept note-5: -A tentative explanation for an observation, phenomenon, or scientific problem is called a hypothesis. While many people outside of science mistakenly think this is called a â€œtheory", a theory instead offers a more comprehensive explanation after several relevant hypotheses have undergone testing.
