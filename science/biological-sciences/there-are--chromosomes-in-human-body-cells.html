There are ____ chromosomes in human body cells.

(A) 45

(B) ** 46

(C) 44

(D) 47

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Humans typically have 23 pairs of chromosomes, or 46 chromosomes in total. Chromosomes are made up of long strands of DNA, which contain all the bodyâ€™s genes.

Concept note-2: -Human cells normally contain 23 pairs of chromosomes, for a total of 46 chromosomes in each cell .

Concept note-3: -The usual number of chromosomes inside every cell of your body is 46 total chromosomes, or 23 pairs. You inherit half of your chromosomes from your biological mother, and the other half from your biological father.

Concept note-4: -In plants and animals (including humans), chromosomes reside in the nucleus of cells. Humans have 22 pairs of numbered chromosomes (autosomes) and one pair of sex chromosomes (XX or XY), for a total of 46.

Concept note-5: -Through the process of fertilization, egg and sperm join to make a cell with 46 chromosomes (23 pairs), called a zygote. For each chromosome pair, one homologous chromosome came from each parent.
