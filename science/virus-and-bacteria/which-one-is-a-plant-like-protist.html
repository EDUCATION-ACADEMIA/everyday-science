Which one is a plant-like protist?

(A) Slime mold

(B) Amoeba

(C) Paramecium

(D) ** Algae

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Plant-like protists are called algae. They include single-celled diatoms and multicellular seaweed. Like plants, algae contain chlorophyll and make food by photosynthesis.

Concept note-2: -Plant-like protists are autotrophs, meaning they make their own food. Plant-like protists include algae, kelp, and seaweed.

Concept note-3: -Ans-: Depending on the type of pigment possessed, plan-like protists are classified under six phyla: Green algae, Brown algae, Red algae, Diatoms, Dinoflagellates, and Euglenoids. Plant-like protists are eukaryotic, photosynthetic, unicellular, or multicellular, possess pyrenoids, gametangia, and a flagellum.

Concept note-4: -Examples of Plant-like Protists. The 7 major groups of algae are red algae, green algae, brown algae, fire algae, golden-brown algae, yellow-green algae, and euglenids.
