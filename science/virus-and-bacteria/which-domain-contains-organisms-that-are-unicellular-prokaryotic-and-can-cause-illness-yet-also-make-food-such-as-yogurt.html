Which domain contains organisms that are unicellular, prokaryotic, and can cause illness yet also make food such as yogurt.

(A) Eukarya

(B) Archaea

(C) ** Bacteria

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Kingdom-Monera : It comprises unicellular, prokaryotic organisms, having no well-defined nucleus and no organelles.

Concept note-2: -The domain Archaea corresponds to the kingdom Archaebacteria. Members of the domain Archaea are unicellular and prokaryotic, and they live in some extreme environments-in volcanic hot springs, brine pools, and black organic mud totally devoid of oxygen. Many of these bacteria can survive only in the absence of oxygen.

Concept note-3: -Body Structure: Eubacteria are single-celled organisms.

Concept note-4: -The Kingdom Fungi belongs to the domain Eukarya and it includes at least 11 separate groups (seven phyla plus four subphyla of the polyphyletic Zygomycota) with diverse genetics, morphologies, and life histories.

Concept note-5: -Statement 1: Kingdom Monera includes unicellular prokaryotic organisms.
