What is one type of the eaters?

(A) Glass Thing Eaters

(B) Metal Thing Eaters

(C) ** Decaying Thing Eaters

(D) ** Bone Thing Eaters

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Eater’s Bone is a pet-summoning item which summons a Baby Eater pet that follows the player around. The Eater’s Bone has a 1/20 (5%) chance of being dropped by the Eater of Worlds. The Baby Eater pet will not teleport to the player when it is far away, unlike most other pets.

Concept note-2: -The Eater’s Bone is an item that summons a Baby Eater of Souls. It is dropped by the Eater of Worlds. The summoned pet follows the player by circling around them, and if the player doesn’t move, it slowly orbits closer.

Concept note-3: -The Eater of Worlds is a pre-Hardmode Corruption-themed worm boss. It is summoned using Worm Food from within The Corruption or Underground Corruption, or by destroying 3 Shadow Orbs that spawn in Corruption worlds. It can be summoned at any time of day.

Concept note-4: -On death, an Eater of Souls has a chance to drop Rotten Chunks which, when combined with Vile Powder at a Demon Altar, creates Worm Food to summon the Eater of Worlds, which is a Corruption-themed boss monster.
