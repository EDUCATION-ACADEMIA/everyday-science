Concept note-1: -In cockroach, the hepatic caeca is present at the junction of the foregut and midgut. It is a ring of 6-8 blind tubules. Their function is to secrete digestive juices to facilitate digestion.

Concept note-2: -At the junction of foregut and midgut, there are 4-6 fingerlike structures called hepatic caeca. They are thin, transparent blind tubules arranged in rosette fashion in the anterior part of the stomach. Their function is to secrete digestive juices.
