True or False? A spider is an insect.

(A) True

(B) ** False

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Final answer: Spiders are not insects.

Concept note-2: -Arachnids are creatures with two body segments, eight legs, no wings or antennae and are not able to chew. Many people think that spiders are insects but they are mistaken since insects have six legs and three main body parts. Most insects have wings. So, spiders are not insects they are Arachnids.

Concept note-3: -Spiders are among the most prevalent household pests, crawling their way into two out of three American homes.

Concept note-4: -Amazing Arthropods Insects, spiders and crustaceans (flies, beetles, ants, ticks, daddy long legs, centipedes, pill bugs, crayfish, etc.) are members of the largest category of creatures on the planet: arthropods. Arthropods have hard, external shells called “exoskeletons, ” segmented bodies and jointed legs.
