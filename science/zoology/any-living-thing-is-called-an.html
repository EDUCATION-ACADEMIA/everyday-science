Any living thing is called an

(A) organ

(B) organization

(C) ** organism

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -An organism refers to a living thing that has an organized structure, can react to stimuli, reproduce, grow, adapt, and maintain homeostasis. An organism would, therefore, be any animal, plant, fungus, protist, bacterium, or archaeon on earth. These organisms may be classified in various ways.

Concept note-2: -any contiguous alive physical entity; entity or being that is living; an individual living thing, such as one animal, plant, fungus, or bacterium. Language.

Concept note-3: -Therefore, a living organism can be defined as anything that has life and consists of cells as its basic unit of organization. Examples of living organisms include humans, fungi, algae, trees, animals, bacteria, protozoa, and insects.

Concept note-4: -: an individual living thing that carries on the activities of life by means of organs which have separate functions but are dependent on each other : a living person, plant, or animal. organismic.
