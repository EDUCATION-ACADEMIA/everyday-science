One of the fishes below is viviparous .Which one is it?

(A) Cod

(B) ** Shark

(C) Mackerel

(D) Flying fish

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Fish Scoliodon is commonly known as Shark. Sharks are aquatic mammals, that reproduce through vivipary.

Concept note-3: -There are over 500 species of shark living in waters around the world and the majority give birth to live young. The remainder are oviparous, meaning they lay eggs.

Concept note-4: -Option B is correct. Throughout the entire gestation cycle, viviparous sharks bear the embryos and give birth to live shark pups, close to how humans give birth.

Concept note-5: -Among the 25.000 species of teleost fishes, approximately 500 fish species are viviparous.
