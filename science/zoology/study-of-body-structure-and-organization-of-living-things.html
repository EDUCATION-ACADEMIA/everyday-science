EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Organs that work together are grouped into organ systems. The organism level is the highest level of organization considered in anatomy/physiology. An organism is a living being that has a cellular structure and that can independently perform all physiologic functions necessary for life.

Concept note-2: -Anatomy is the science that studies the structure of the body.

Concept note-3: -Anatomy is the area of science that deals with the structure of a life form, also called an organism. This includes its external appearance and features as well as its internal ones. Simply put, the study of anatomy is the study of an organismâ€™s form and parts.

Concept note-4: -Life processes of the human body are maintained at several levels of structural organization. These include the chemical, cellular, tissue, organ, organ system, and the organism level. Higher levels of organization are built from lower levels.

Concept note-5: -The study of the human body is called anatomy. A scientist who studies anatomy is called an anatomist. Zoologists also study the anatomies of other animals.
