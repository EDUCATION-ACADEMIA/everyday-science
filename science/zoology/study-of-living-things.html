study of living things

(A) zoology

(B) taxonomy

(C) ** biology

(D) limnology

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Biology is a branch of science that deals with living organisms and their vital processes. Biology encompasses diverse fields, including botany, conservation, ecology, evolution, genetics, marine biology, medicine, microbiology, molecular biology, physiology, and zoology.

Concept note-2: -Biology explores the interconnectedness of all living things and the environment. Through learning in biology, students discover that life involves interactions at all levels of organisation: cells interact with their environment and with each other, so do organs, organisms and ecosystems.

Concept note-3: -For this reason, living things are called organisms. The activities of the cells are controlled by the cellâ€™s genetic material-its DNA. In some types of organisms, called eukaryotes, the DNA is contained within a membrane-bound structure called the nucleus.

Concept note-4: -The correct option is C. Life science. Life science deals with all living organisms of this universe. It has two branches: Botany and Zoology. Botany is a branch of life science which deals with plant life, whereas zoology is that branch of life science dealing with animal life.
