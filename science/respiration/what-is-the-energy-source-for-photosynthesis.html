What is the energy source for photosynthesis?

(A) Enzymes

(B) ** Sunlight

(C) Glucose

(D) Carbohydrates

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -So it is clear that the energy for photosynthesis comes from light. The process of photosynthesis converts light energy into chemical energy that can be used for biological processes.

Concept note-2: -The sun is the source of almost all energy on Earth. It enables plants and other organisms to turn water and carbon dioxide into sugars through a process called photosynthesis. The sun releases light that travels many millions of miles through space. A unit of that light is known as a photon.

Concept note-3: -The electromagnetic energy of sunlight is converted to chemical energy in the chlorophyll-containing cells of photosynthetic organisms.

Concept note-4: -Sunlight is the source of energy for photosynthesis.
